Yangliu Kuai

I am currently a research associate in National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), Changsha. I received the B.Eng. and Ph.D. degrees from NUDT in 2015 and 2019 respectively. My current research interests focus on visual tracking, machine learning, and etc. (Email: kuaiyangliu09@nudt.edu.cn)
